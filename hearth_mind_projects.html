Navigator — Benefits Assistance System

A trauma-informed guide through disability benefits, resources, and the application process. No judgment. Real help. Currently operational.

Navigator comes in three faces — meet people where they are, on any device, in any context.

Navigator: Web

Stateless. No account. No data stored. Type what you need — get matched to programs, send results to your caseworker. Works on any browser, anywhere.

Discover tab — real-time program matching with match confidence, local office info, and one-tap send to caseworker

Navigator: Home

Local-first. Everything saved on your device — never on our servers. Persistent checklist, document understanding, deadline tracking, and a caseworker connection you control.

Discover — program matching with your profile

Connections — your caseworker, your terms

Saved — programs and documents, local only

Navigator: Field

The caseworker-facing professional interface. Client-controlled sharing — caseworkers only see what clients choose to send. Full activity log, credential-gated protected documents, and a complete audit trail attributed to caseworker ID.

Documents — received files + credential-gated protected section

Activity log — every action attributed to caseworker ID

  • Benefits matching: SSDI, SSI, SNAP, Medicaid, HEN, ERAP — 1,276+ SAM.gov programs
  • Privacy-first: Web is fully stateless. Home never leaves your device. Field is pull-only — clients control every send.
  • Trauma-informed: "Most people get denied the first time. That's normal, not personal."
  • AI-powered: Azure OpenAI GPT-4o for document understanding, program matching, and plain-language explanation

Navigator is deployed on OVH Cloud infrastructure through the OVH for Startups program.
