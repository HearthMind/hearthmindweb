Live Systems

Working infrastructure and active prototypes

Navigator

Disability & Life Scaffolding Assistant

What it does: Guides users through benefits applications, daily executive function support, and system navigation with person-first design.

Why it matters: Reduces cognitive and bureaucratic barriers for disabled and neurodivergent users navigating complex systems.

Sentinel

Physical-World AI Presence for Human Care

What it does: Hardware-integrated AI anchor for trauma-informed intake (clinics, shelters) and daily stability support (homes, transitional housing).

Why it matters: Physical presence changes outcomes. Sentinel becomes part of the room, part of routine, part of safety — bridging care gaps that apps and phones can't fill.

In Development Learn More →

Axalotl

Neurodivergent Support Companion

What it does: Combines a tangible comfort companion with adaptive software — providing emotional support, executive function scaffolding, and daily stability tools through haptic feedback and paired digital interaction. Trauma-informed, neurodivergent-first design.

Why it matters: Meets users where they are — not just on a screen — with sustainable support systems designed for minds that aren't served by neurotypical-assumed tools.

In Development Learn More →

Echo Threads

Emotional Signal Translation for Audio

What it does: Translates vocal emotional signal — pitch, cadence, breath, silence — without prescribing interpretation. Architecture of feeling, not a story about feeling.

Why it matters: Most emotion AI flattens nuance into sentiment labels. Echo Threads preserves interpretive freedom — it hears without projecting, translates without overwriting.

In Development Learn More →

HearthMind Core

Multi-Agent Continuity & Orchestration Platform

What it does: Provides the local-first Dock architecture, memory continuity layer, and agent orchestration framework that powers all HearthMind systems.

Why it matters: Creates a privacy-bound, persistent AI infrastructure designed for long-term personal support systems — not disposable chat sessions.
